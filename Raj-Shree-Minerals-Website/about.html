About Raj Shree Mineral

Welcome to Raj Shree Mineral

Established 1994, Raj Shree Mineral is a Rajasthan-based manufacturer, exporter, and supplier of industrial minerals.

We are a one-stop destination for acquiring pure and natural minerals products and compounds. Our company has earned the reputation of a prestigious manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Limestone, Hydrated Lime, Quick Lime Powder & other minerals.

The positive attribute of our company is that we have our mines and processing units that ensures supply of pure and natural minerals.
